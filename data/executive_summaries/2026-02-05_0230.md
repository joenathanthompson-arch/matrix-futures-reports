## Key Macro Themes
- Risk-off sentiment (VIX >20) is a dominant theme, supporting safe-haven assets like gold and the Japanese Yen while pressuring risk-sensitive currencies like the Australian Dollar.
- Improving credit conditions, with narrowing credit spreads and a steepening yield curve, are providing a strong underpinning for equities, particularly for small-cap stocks.
- A broadly weaker US Dollar is providing a tailwind for commodities and most foreign currencies.
