- **BOJ Normalization as the Structural JPY Theme:** The Bank of Japan's multi-year journey from ultra-loose policy toward normalization is the most significant structural FX theme. With the BOJ expected to reach 1.00% by mid-2026 and the Fed holding or cutting, the USD/JPY carry trade is unwinding, driving sustained JPY strength.
