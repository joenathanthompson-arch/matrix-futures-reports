## Key Macro Themes

1. **The Real Yield Tailwind**: The 10-year TIPS yield (DFII10) has declined ~11bps to 1.89%, reducing the opportunity cost of holding non-yielding assets (gold, silver) and compressing equity discount rates. This is the single most powerful bullish driver in the current model.

2. **Iran War: Bifurcated Impact**: The ongoing conflict drives a geopolitical risk premium into oil (WTI $90.77) and gold (safe-haven demand) while simultaneously creating a risk-off environment (VIX 27.19, MOVE +41% monthly) that is damaging for commodity-linked currencies like AUD. The inventory build (+3.8M barrels) provides a partial bearish offset to the crude oil supply premium.

3. **Divergent Central Bank Paths**: The RBA hikes (March 17), the ECB holds with a hawkish tilt (March 19), and the Fed holds with near-certainty (March 18-19). The BoJ continues its structural tightening cycle. These divergences create meaningful FX opportunities, particularly in 6J (bullish) and 6A (bearish).
