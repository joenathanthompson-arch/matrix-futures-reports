## Key Macro Themes

The dominant macro theme is the intersection of falling real yields and the Iran war supply shock. Real yields have declined 16 basis points over five days as markets price in slower growth and potential Fed easing later in 2026, creating a powerful tailwind for both gold and long-duration equity assets simultaneously. This unusual alignment — where both safe havens and risk assets are bullish — is explained by the second theme: the Iran war is simultaneously creating a supply-side inflation shock (bullish commodities, gold) and a demand-side growth scare (bullish bonds, lower real yields). The third theme is the BoJ's hawkish pivot, which represents the most significant structural shift in the FX space, with JPY poised to strengthen as the rate differential with the USD narrows for the first time in years.
