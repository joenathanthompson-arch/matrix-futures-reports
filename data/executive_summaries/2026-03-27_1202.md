## Overall Market Bias: BEARISH

The macro picture remains defensive. A firmer U.S. dollar, a VIX still near 30, and only-stable U.S. growth are keeping pressure on equities, metals, and pro-cyclical FX, while crude oil retains geopolitical support and the yen remains the cleaner defensive FX expression.

The Fed stayed on hold, the ECB also held, the RBA delivered a March hike, and the BoJ kept policy restrictive enough to preserve a tightening bias; however, the market is still trading the stronger-dollar, higher-volatility regime first.

### 6J (Japanese Yen): +2 SLIGHT_BULLISH (6/10)

That said, the absolute U.S.-Japan rate gap remains wide, so the bullish case is cleaner tactically than structurally. Bullish 6J still means a softer USD/JPY rather than a broad risk-on FX view.
