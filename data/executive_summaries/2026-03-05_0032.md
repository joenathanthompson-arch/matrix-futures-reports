The rate differential between the BoJ and the Fed is narrowing systematically, and the safe-haven demand from the Iran conflict amplifies the structural JPY appreciation thesis. The March 18-19 BoJ meeting is the key catalyst — a hike would push the 6J score to +7 or higher.
