## Key Macro Themes

1. **Falling Real Yields as Structural Tailwind:** The DFII10 10-year TIPS yield has declined approximately 15bps to 1.80%, reducing the opportunity cost of holding non-yielding assets like gold and supporting equity valuations through lower discount rates. This is the dominant cross-asset driver today.

2. **VIX Surge Signals Risk-Off Rotation:** The VIX has jumped +10% to 21.01, its highest level since early February, as all major US equity indices decline 1-1.7%. This risk-off environment creates a bifurcated market where safe-haven assets (gold, JPY) benefit while risk-sensitive assets (AUD, small caps) face headwinds.

3. **BoJ Hawkish Escalation Drives JPY Strength:** Multiple BoJ officials and ex-policymakers have signaled that a rate hike as early as March 2026 is possible, with up to three hikes expected in 2026. The narrowing JPY-USD rate differential is the strongest FX catalyst in today's report.
