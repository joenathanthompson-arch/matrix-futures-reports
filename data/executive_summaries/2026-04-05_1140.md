## Key Macro Themes

1. **Geopolitical Risk Premium**: The ongoing US-Iran conflict is keeping the VIX elevated (>20) and providing a persistent bid to safe havens like Gold and the Japanese Yen, while complicating the picture for crude oil.
2. **Slowing US Growth**: The Atlanta Fed GDPNow estimate has fallen to 1.6% for Q1 2026. This "bad news" is being interpreted as "good news" for equities, as it cements expectations for Fed rate cuts, particularly benefiting the Nasdaq.
3. **Central Bank Divergence**: While the Fed remains on a neutral hold, both the BoJ and ECB are exhibiting hawkish tendencies. This divergence is weakening the US Dollar against the Yen and Euro.
