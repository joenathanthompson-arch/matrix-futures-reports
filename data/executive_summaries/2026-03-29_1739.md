## Key Macro Themes

Rising real yields are the dominant macro force of this reporting period, with 10-year TIPS yields climbing 20 basis points in just five trading days to reach 2.08%, directly compressing valuations across gold, equities, and risk assets. The US/Israel-Iran war, now entering its second month since escalating on February 28, 2026, has created a complex macro environment: geopolitical risk supports crude oil and safe-haven assets in theory, but the practical reality is that institutional liquidity needs are overwhelming safe-haven demand, as evidenced by the historic $12 billion in gold ETF outflows during March. The ECB's hawkish pivot at its March 19 meeting stands out as a divergence from the global risk-off narrative, creating a relative value opportunity in EUR-denominated assets.
