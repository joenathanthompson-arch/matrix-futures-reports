## Key Macro Themes
- **Falling Real Yields & Weak USD:** This combination is the primary driver of the current risk-on environment, providing a strong tailwind for both equities (especially tech) and precious metals.
- **Policy Divergence in FX:** The Bank of Japan's move towards tighter policy stands in contrast to the neutral stances of the Fed and ECB, creating a clear driver for Yen strength.
- **Goldilocks Growth:** The economic data suggests a 'Goldilocks' scenario of stable, solid growth without being so strong as to trigger fears of Fed hawkishness, supporting equity markets.
