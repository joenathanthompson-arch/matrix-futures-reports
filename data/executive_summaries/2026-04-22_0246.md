## Key Macro Themes

- The dollar is easing at the same time that U.S. growth expectations are cooling, creating a constructive backdrop for commodities and selected non-USD FX exposure.
- Crude oil remains the standout macro trade because supply tightening, inventory draws, and geopolitical risk are aligned in the same direction.
- Equity signals remain fragmented rather than broadly bullish because wider credit spreads and firmer volatility are offsetting the otherwise supportive impulse from dollar weakness.
