## Methodology

This report uses a quantitative scoring system that aggregates multiple macro factors relevant to each instrument:

### Macro Factors Analyzed

1. **Fed Stance:** Monetary policy direction (Dovish/Neutral/Hawkish)
2. **Real Yields:** 10Y TIPS yield trend
3. **USD Strength:** Dollar index (DXY) direction
4. **VIX:** Equity volatility (risk appetite indicator)
5. **Growth Narrative:** Economic growth trajectory (GDPNow)
6. **Credit Spreads:** High-yield OAS (credit conditions)
7. **Yield Curve:** 2s10s spread (economic expectations)
8. **SOX Index:** Semiconductor sector (tech health)
9. **MOVE Index:** Bond volatility (rate uncertainty)
10. **Copper:** Industrial demand indicator
11. **Oil Supply/Inventories:** Energy market balance
12. **Gold ETF Flows:** Safe-haven demand
13. **Geopolitical Risk:** Global stability assessment
14. **ECB Policy:** European monetary policy (for FX)

### Scoring System

- Each factor is assigned a value: -1 (bearish), 0 (neutral), or +1 (bullish)
- Factors are weighted based on their relevance to each instrument
- Final scores are aggregated and interpreted as:
  - **Score > +0.5:** BULLISH
  - **Score -0.5 to +0.5:** NEUTRAL
  - **Score < -0.5:** BEARISH

### Data Sources

- Federal Reserve (Fed Funds Rate, GDPNow)
- FRED (Real Yields, Credit Spreads, Yield Curve)
- CME FedWatch Tool (Rate expectations)
- CBOE (VIX)
- TradingView (DXY, SOX, MOVE)
- EIA (Oil inventories)
- World Gold Council (ETF flows)
- Multiple news sources (Geopolitical risk)
