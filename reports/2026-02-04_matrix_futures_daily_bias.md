## Data & Methodology

This report is generated using a proprietary quantitative model that analyzes over 14 real-time macroeconomic data points. Each data point is scored on a scale of -2 (very bearish) to +2 (very bullish) based on its deviation from a neutral baseline. These scores are then weighted according to their historical impact on each specific futures instrument to produce a final directional bias score. The confidence score reflects the level of agreement between the various macro factors.
