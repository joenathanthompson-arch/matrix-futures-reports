## Key Macro Themes
- **Central Bank Divergence:** The RBA's surprise hike and the BoJ's hawkish tilt are creating clear divergence against a neutral Fed and ECB, driving strength in AUD and JPY.
- **Supportive Credit Conditions:** Tight high-yield credit spreads continue to provide a tailwind for equities, particularly for smaller, more credit-sensitive companies in the Russell 2000.
- **USD Strength as a Headwind:** A rising US Dollar is putting pressure on commodities and creating a mixed picture for US equities.
