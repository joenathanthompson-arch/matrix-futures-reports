## Methodology Notes


### Scoring System

- **Normalized Score Range:** -10 (strong bearish) to +10 (strong bullish)
- **Calculation:** (Raw Weighted Score / Max Possible Weight) × 10
- **Factor Scores:** Each macro factor assigned -1 (bearish), 0 (neutral), or +1 (bullish)
- **Weights:** Factors weighted by importance to each instrument (typically 1-3)

### Bias Labels

- **+7 to +10:** STRONG BULLISH
- **+4 to +6.9:** BULLISH
- **+1 to +3.9:** SLIGHTLY BULLISH
- **-0.9 to +0.9:** NEUTRAL
- **-1 to -3.9:** SLIGHTLY BEARISH
- **-4 to -6.9:** BEARISH
- **-7 to -10:** STRONG BEARISH

### Signal Decay

Bias scores are most relevant for the current trading session. High-conviction signals (+7 or higher) typically have a shorter time horizon (1-5 trading sessions) before reassessment is needed.
