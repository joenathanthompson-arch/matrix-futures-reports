The BoJ is on a tightening path (with rate hikes expected in June and beyond), while the Fed is on hold. This is causing the interest rate differential to narrow, which is bullish for the yen.
*   **Entry:** Look to buy JPY futures (6J) on any weakness.
*   **Target:** The yen is likely to appreciate against the US dollar in the coming weeks.
*   **Stop-Loss:** A surprise dovish turn from the BoJ or a hawkish surprise from the Fed would negate this trade idea.
