# Data Sources and Citations

**Date:** February 14, 2026

This document provides a list of all data sources used to generate the Matrix Futures Daily Bias Report.

## Macroeconomic Factors

-   **Fed Stance:** CME FedWatch Tool - [https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html)
-   **Real Yields (10Y TIPS):** FRED, 10-Year Treasury Inflation-Indexed Security, Constant Maturity (DFII10) - [https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/DFII10](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/DFII10)
-   **Real Yields (Fallback):** CNBC, U.S. 10 Year TIPS Bond - [https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/US10YTIP](https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/US10YTIP)
-   **USD (DXY):** TradingView, U.S. Dollar Currency Index (DXY) - [https://www.tradingview.com/symbols/TVC-DXY/](https://www.tradingview.com/symbols/TVC-DXY/)
-   **VIX (Volatility):** CNBC, CBOE Volatility Index (.VIX) - [https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/.VIX](https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/.VIX)
-   **Growth Narrative (GDPNow):** Federal Reserve Bank of Atlanta, GDPNow - [https://www.atlantafed.org/cqer/research/gdpnow](https://www.atlantafed.org/cqer/research/gdpnow)
-   **Credit Spreads (HY OAS):** FRED, ICE BofA US High Yield Index Option-Adjusted Spread (BAMLH0A0HYM2) - [https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/BAMLH0A0HYM2](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/BAMLH0A0HYM2)
-   **SOX (Semiconductors):** TradingView, Philadelphia Semiconductor Index (SOX) - [https://www.tradingview.com/symbols/SOX/](https://www.tradingview.com/symbols/SOX/)
-   **MOVE (Rate Volatility):** TradingView, ICE BofAML U.S. Bond Market Option Volatility Estimate Index (MOVE) - [https://www.tradingview.com/symbols/TVC-MOVE/](https://www.tradingview.com/symbols/TVC-MOVE/)
-   **2s10s Yield Curve:** FRED, 10-Year Treasury Constant Maturity Minus 2-Year Treasury Constant Maturity (T10Y2Y) - [https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/T10Y2Y](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/T10Y2Y)
-   **Copper:** Investing.com, Copper Futures - [https://www.investing.com/commodities/copper](https://www.investing.com/commodities/copper)
-   **Gold ETF Flows:** World Gold Council, Gold ETF Holdings and Flows - [https://www.gold.org/goldhub/research/gold-etfs-holdings-and-flows/2026/02](https://www.gold.org/goldhub/research/gold-etfs-holdings-and-flows/2026/02)
-   **Oil Inventories (EIA):** U.S. Energy Information Administration, Weekly Petroleum Status Report - [https://www.eia.gov/petroleum/supply/weekly/](https://www.eia.gov/petroleum/supply/weekly/)
-   **Geopolitical Risk:** General news search and analysis from Reuters, The Guardian, and other sources.
-   **ECB Policy Stance:** European Central Bank, Monetary policy decisions - [https://www.ecb.europa.eu/press/pr/date/2026/html/ecb.mp260205~001d26959b.en.html](https://www.ecb.europa.eu/press/pr/date/2026/html/ecb.mp260205~001d26959b.en.html)
-   **RBA Policy Stance:** Reserve Bank of Australia, Monetary Policy Decision - [https://www.rba.gov.au/monetary-policy/int-rate-decisions/2026/](https://www.rba.gov.au/monetary-policy/int-rate-decisions/2026/)
-   **BoJ Policy Stance:** Bank of Japan, Monetary Policy Releases - [https://www.boj.or.jp/en/mopo/mpmdeci/mpr_2026/index.htm](https://www.boj.or.jp/en/mopo/mpmdeci/mpr_2026/index.htm)
-   **China Growth (PMI):** National Bureau of Statistics of China & S&P Global/RatingDog - [https://www.stats.gov.cn/english/PressRelease/202602/t20260202_1962478.html](https://www.stats.gov.cn/english/PressRelease/202602/t20260202_1962478.html)
